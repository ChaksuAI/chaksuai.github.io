Last-Mile Vision Care,
Powered by AI.

ChákṣuAI is an open-source platform designed to help ophthalmologists diagnose and manage eye diseases. Using deep learning, it analyzes retinal images to provide accurate classifications, segmentations, and risk predictions.

How It Works

1. Upload Retinal Images

Securely upload patient data, including fundus photographs and Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) scans.

2. AI-Powered Analysis

Our deep learning models automatically classify diseases, detect abnormalities, and perform detailed segmentations.

3. Receive Actionable Insights

Get clear, visual results and data-driven reports to support your diagnostic workflow and treatment planning.

Core Features

  • Disease Classification: Automatically identify conditions like diabetic retinopathy, glaucoma, and AMD from images.
  • Abnormality Detection: Pinpoint key biomarkers such as microaneurysms, haemorrhages, and exudates.
  • Retinal Segmentation: Precisely segment anatomical structures like the optic disc and cup for detailed analysis.
  • OCT Fluid Analysis: Perform volumetric analysis of fluid in Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) images.
  • Risk Prediction: Assess the risk of disease progression to aid in proactive patient management.
  • Open-Source & Collaborative: A community-driven platform that is transparent, extensible, and free to use.
